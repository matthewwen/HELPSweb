Welcome to Purdue HELPS Laboratory¶

High-Efficiency and Low-Power Systems (HELPS)¶

Mission: This research team aims to create leaders.¶

This team aims to create leaders that can recognize the world’s needs and develop solutions

The High-Efficiency, Low-Power System (HELPS) Laboratory in the Purdue develops the technology to improve the efficiency and performance of computing systems. Our research includes computer vision, machine learning, cloud and mobile computing, embedded systems software engineering, as well as applications.
